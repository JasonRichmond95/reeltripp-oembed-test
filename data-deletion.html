Data Deletion Request

If you have interacted with Reeltripp through our website or connected via a third-party platform (such as Facebook or Instagram), you may request the deletion of your personal data.

How to request deletion

Send an email to hello@reeltripp.com with the subject line Data Deletion Request and include:

  • Your full name
  • The email address or account used with Reeltripp
  • Any relevant reference (e.g., booking ID, content link)

What happens next

We will respond within 30 days to confirm deletion or explain if we are legally required to keep certain information.
